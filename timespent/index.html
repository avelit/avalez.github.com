Flexible time tracking for BitBucket.

Time Spent Add-On is a modern and yet immature version of Timesheet Reports and Gadgets Add-On. It is intended to provide flexible and extensible solution. It is designed to evolve faster and is built specifically for Atlassian Add-on.

Install

Just go to Profile - Add-ons - search for 'timespent', and click Add.
