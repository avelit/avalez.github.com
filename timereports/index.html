Overview

Time Reports Add-On is a modern and yet immature version of Timesheet Reports and Gadgets Add-On. It is intended to provide flexible and extensible solution. It is designed to evolve faster and is built specifically for JIRA Cloud.

Install

Just go to Administration - Plugins - Install in your JIRA, search for 'timerports', see 'Time Reports Add-On for JIRA Cloud' and click install.

When installed Time Reports Add-On adds Issues - Time Reports menu item under Timesheet menu section. It gives access to three report types at the moment:

  • Time spent on Issues by Days - old time sheet view.
  • Time spent on Issues by Users - old project pivot view.
  • Time estimate vs actual - example of straightforward extension to Time Reports model.

More reports may be added upon interest.
