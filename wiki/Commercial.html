Commercial

Since version 2.3.4 plugin is available on Atlassian Marketplace as Paid, with price $10 - $600 depending on number of users in your JIRA.

Note, it is still open and free for open-source projects. You can request free open-source license similarly to Atlassian products here, see Marketplace Add-on option.

You can try/buy timesheet plugin on Atlassian marketplace or from Jira itself, see this video for example.

However, it's not possible to buy it directly in a few countries (mainly India and Croatia) from this exception list. Please, contact one of the Atlassian Experts listed on this page instead, who can help you facilitate the purchase.

You have to buy license for continued use, although there are no major functionality restrictions with no license, you'll see UNREGISTERED all the time and few things will not be available:

  1. Plugin Configuration in Administration -> Add-Ons
  2. Subscribtions to Timesheet Report), to receive it by email
  3. Worked Time Chart
  4. Timesheet Holidays
  5. Plugin updates notification.

At any case, you can vote or request new features here.
